About me

I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Master's degree in Mathematical Computer Science in 2012 and 2014, respectively, at Ghent University. I pursued a PhD at the IPI research group at Ghent University and the DAMBI research group at VIB and obtained my degree in 2019. I am currently working as a postdoctoral researcher at VIB on machine learning techniques that can automate the biological image analysis pipeline.

ISBI paper accepted!

Our paper entitled "Domain Adaptive Segmentation in Volume Electron Microscopy Imaging" has been accepted for an oral presentation at the ISBI conference. In this work, we introduce an ergonomical domain adaptation technique for segmentation that achieves equal to better performance compared to the state-of-the-art.
