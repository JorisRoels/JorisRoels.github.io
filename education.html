Education

From 2015 until 2018, I have been assisting prof. Wilfried Philips, prof. Bart Goossens and prof. Aleksandra Pizurica with Image Processing and Artificial Intelligence courses. I am currently teaching several lectures in the Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence courses of prof. Yvan Saeys. I have also (co-)supervised several Master thesis students in Engineering and Computer Science.

Courses

Machine Learning (prof. Yvan Saeys)

This course handles both the basics in Machine Learning (feature selection, clustering, classification and regression) and more advanced material such as reinforcement learning and deep learning.

Artificial Intelligence (prof. Yvan Saeys)

This course prepares the student to determine when an AI approach is appropriate for a given problem, identify the appropriate representation and reasoning mechanism, and implement and evaluate it.

Image Processing (prof. Wilfried Philips & prof. Bart Goossens)

This is an introductory course that gives an overview of the most important principles and techniques in image and video processing and the applications in which they are used. Mathematical backgrounds and technical details are also discussed.

Artificial Intelligence (prof. Aleksandra Pizurica)

The course gives an overview of the principles and modern approaches in artificial intelligence. The focus is on intelligent agents, reasoning under uncertainty, and making rational decisions.
